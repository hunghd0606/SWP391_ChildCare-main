    A little bit about us

    After more than 10 years of pioneering the application and spreading of Montessori education methods in Vietnam, YS ChildCare is highly appreciated by parents and Montessori experts in applying the standard Montessori educational method, bringing to the environment. Personalized learning to unleash the inherent potential in each child.

    With an understanding of the psychophysiological characteristics of children in the period 0-6 years old, combined with subtle observations and daily notes, YS ChildCare safety and health teachers develop a personal development roadmap for each child to support each child. Support children in the most timely manner as well as provide dedicated care, taking care of each child's meal and sleep.

    Vietnam's most trusted
    child care resource

    The international preschool system YS ChildCare was established in May 2022, pioneering in applying the Montessori educational philosophy to teaching, laying the foundation for the development of the Montessori method in Vietnam.

    Currently, ChildCare safety system has facilities in: Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh, Quang Ninh, ... with a series of classrooms designed in an open style to make the most of natural light and air. The spacious and comfortable function rooms ensure safety standards and meet the learning and living needs of Students.

    Our mission

    YS ChildCare is committed to providing the community with a sustainable educational environment, inspiring creativity.

    Here at YS ChildCare, we feel privileged to share your child's early years and we are committed to making sure that this is the right place for your child and for you. We fully recognise the great trust that you have placed in us when deciding to leave your child here and we will work hard to ensure that we repay that trust.

    Our goals

    Our carers provide genuine love and concern for the children in their care, thus providing them with a warm, friendly and inviting atmosphere where your child will feel secure and happy in their new environment.

    We want every part of our service to not only meet, but to exceed your needs and those of your child. This requires partnership, so aT YS ChildCare we believe that families and educators must cooperate and work together, sharing information and building relationships and a sense of community and belonging.

    Things to do
